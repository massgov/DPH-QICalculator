Do you have symptoms?

If you tested positive for COVID-19, you should stay home and isolate for 5 days from when your symptoms began. If your symptoms have improved after 5 days at home, you may leave your house and should continue to wear a mask for an additional 5 days.
(5 days home, 5 days masking.)
If your symptoms have continued after 5 days, you should continue to stay home until your fever resolves without the use of medication like ibuprofen or Tylenol, and your symptoms have improved.
You should continue to wear a mask around others for 10 days after the onset of symptoms . If you are unable to mask, you should continue to isolate at home for an additional 5 days (10 days total).

If you tested positive for COVID-19 and do not have symptoms, you should stay home and isolate for 5 days from the date of your positive test.
After 5 days, you may leave your house and should continue to wear a mask around others for an additional 5 days (5 days at home, 5 days masking).
